The Hackathon Website That Wasn't
My friends and I wanted to build a virtual space tour website. We had no idea how to make a webpage. What came out looked nothing like the plan — and that was the whole lesson. We learned that the gap between the idea in your head and the thing you actually build is where all the real work lives. We also learned that laughing through the chaos makes it worth it. I'd do it again.
